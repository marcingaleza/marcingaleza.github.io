About me

I’m Marcin Gałęza, a seasoned Product Designer & UI Developer with over 20 years of experience crafting elegant, performant user interfaces. My background spans a wide range of digital products, with a strong emphasis on usability, scalability, and design consistency.

In recent years, I’ve focused on developing and maintaining Design Systems using tools like React, Vue, Storybook, and TailwindCSS—bridging the gap between design and front-end engineering. I bring a deep UX mindset paired with the technical skills to bring ideas to life in code.

I’m currently open to new opportunities in product-focused roles where I can:

  • Build and scale design systems
  • Improve interface quality and accessibility
  • Collaborate closely with designers and engineers
  • Create seamless handoff between design and development

If you’re looking for a hybrid designer-developer who values clarity, performance, and craft, I’d love to hear from you.
